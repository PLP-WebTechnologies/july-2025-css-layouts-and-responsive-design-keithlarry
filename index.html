Biography

Sasuke Uchiha is a former Leaf Village ninja, a member of the powerful Uchiha clan, and one of the most skilled shinobi of his generation. Driven by revenge and later redemption, his journey is marked by intense battles, emotional conflicts, and incredible growth.

Quick Facts

  • Clan: Uchiha
  • Village: Hidden Leaf
  • Eye Power: Sharingan & Rinnegan
  • Team: Team 7
  • Brother: Itachi Uchiha
